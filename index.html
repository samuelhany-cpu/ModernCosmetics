Our History

The Birth of Modern Cosmatics:

Modern Cosmatics was born out of a visionary's dream in the bustling streets of Paris in the early 2000s. Marie Leclerc, a talented cosmetic chemist with a passion for innovation, envisioned a cosmetics brand that would marry scientific excellence with artistic creativity.

2005 - 2010: The Foundation Years

In 2005, armed with her expertise and determination, Marie founded Modern Cosmatics in a small laboratory tucked away in the heart of Paris. With a focus on research and development, Marie and her small team worked tirelessly to formulate skincare products that combined cutting-edge science with luxurious ingredients.


As word of their breakthrough formulations spread, Modern Cosmatics quickly gained recognition within the industry and garnered a loyal following among beauty enthusiasts. Their commitment to quality and innovation set them apart, laying the foundation for future success.

2010 - 2015: Expansion and Recognition

Buoyed by their early achievements, Modern Cosmatics embarked on a period of expansion. Their product line grew to include makeup products, each meticulously crafted to enhance natural beauty and inspire self-expression.


During this time, Modern Cosmatics caught the attention of beauty editors and influencers worldwide, earning accolades for their innovative formulas and chic packaging. Their iconic products became must-haves in the beauty routines of discerning consumers around the globe.

2015 - Present: A Global Phenomenon

In the years that followed, Modern Cosmatics cemented its position as a global leader in the cosmetics industry. With a commitment to sustainability and inclusivity, the brand continued to push boundaries, introducing groundbreaking products that catered to a diverse range of skin tones and concerns.


From red carpet appearances to everyday essentials, Modern Cosmatics became synonymous with beauty excellence, empowering individuals to express themselves authentically through the transformative power of cosmetics.


Today, as Modern Cosmatics looks toward the future, their dedication to innovation, quality, and inclusivity remains unwavering. With a global presence and a passionate community of supporters, they continue to redefine beauty standards and inspire confidence in every individual they touch.
